Data Visualizations



The following visualizations present the quantity and quality of the vast amount of data we have extracted from Reddit to produce our recommender.

Pie Chart: Subreddits by category and number of posts in that subreddit under that category

Bar Chart: Similarity between recommended subreddits to a specific subreddit 'news'

Line Graph: relationship between the number of comments and reddit scores

Image Montage: a glance of the images from posts with the most comments in December 2014

Word Cloud: the most popular words from user comments

Scatter Plot: number of comments vs. scores from posts with the highest reddit scores

Starburst Graph: posts with the highest number of comments
